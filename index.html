Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our sacco

How do I become a member of JB's 100 Sacco?
Simply purchase at least one share at 100K. Visit our offices or contact us online to start the registration process. You'll need valid ID, proof of income, and completion of our membership form. Once approved, you can start enjoying all member benefits immediately.
What's the difference between regular shares and Phase 3 investment?
Regular shares cost 100K per share and give you access to all basic sacco services, dividends, and farm programs. Phase 3 is an 8M investment package for serious investors, offering exclusive project participation, leadership opportunities, priority returns, and involvement in major agricultural ventures.
How often are dividends paid?
Dividends are calculated and paid annually based on the sacco's performance and your level of participation. Our agricultural investments typically generate returns of 25-35% annually, which are shared among members proportionally.
Can I access loans as a new member?
Yes! After 6 months of active membership and regular savings, you can access loans up to 3x your total savings at competitive interest rates. Emergency loans are available sooner with flexible repayment terms.
What happens if I want to withdraw my shares?
Members can withdraw shares with a 90-day notice period. This allows us to manage our agricultural investments properly. Your shares will be valued at current market value, and you'll receive your principal plus any accrued dividends.
Do I need farming experience to join?
Not at all! We provide comprehensive training and expert support for all members. Our team handles the day-to-day farm management, and you receive regular updates and can participate in farm visits. Many of our successful members started with zero farming experience.
